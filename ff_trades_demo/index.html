FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you charge a call-out fee?
In most cases, no. If your job is outside our usual area or requires specialist equipment, we’ll tell you before attending.
Are you Gas Safe registered?
Yes. Gas work is completed by Gas Safe registered engineers, and you can request our registration number on booking.
Can you provide a quote before starting work?
Yes. We’ll explain options and provide a clear quote before any work begins, so you’re never surprised.
Do you work with landlords?
Yes. We handle repairs, upgrades and servicing for rental properties, including scheduled appointments and straightforward invoicing.
