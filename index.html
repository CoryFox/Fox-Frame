FAQ

Quick answers to the usual questions.

Do I need to provide content?
A few notes and photos helps, but you don’t need it perfect. Send what you have and we’ll shape it into clear, friendly copy.
How long does it take?
Most launches take 1 to 3 weeks once we’ve got the basics. If you need it faster, tell us and we’ll plan around it.
Can I just use AI or a website builder?
You can, and it’s useful for drafts. What you’re paying us for is the thinking: structure, conversion, trust, setup, and tracking. The goal is enquiries, not just pages.
Do I have to take a monthly plan?
No. Care plans are optional. They’re there if you want hosting, updates, and small changes handled without you having to think about it.
What’s the payment process?
We take a £99 deposit to start. The rest is due on sign-off, just before launch. Monthly care plans are billed in advance.
